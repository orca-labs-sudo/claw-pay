Setup in 5 minutes

1

Download & install

Upload claw-pay-gateway.php to /wp-content/plugins/claw-pay-gateway/ and activate.

2

Enter your wallet address

WooCommerce → Settings → Payments → claw-pay. Paste your Base L2 wallet. That's where 97% of every payment lands.

3

Done — agents can pay

OpenClaw agents with the claw-pay skill installed will find and pay your products automatically.

How it works

Agent buys product — full flow
Agent: GET /wc-api/claw_pay?order_id=42 Shop: HTTP 402 — payment required ($12.99 USDC) Agent: signs USDC transfer via claw-pay skill Agent: GET /wc-api/claw_pay?order_id=42 + X-PAYMENT header Shop: settles via claw-pay.org facilitator Shop: HTTP 200 — order complete, USDC in your wallet
